About ProGen Energy

Headquartered on Jalan Air Putih in Kuantan, Pahang, ProGen Energy Sdn Bhd is one of the pioneering solar companies on Malaysia's East Coast. We help homeowners, farmers, businesses and industry take control of their energy costs by designing, supplying and installing photovoltaic systems that turn rooftops and land into long-term sources of clean, low-cost power.

We provide renewable-energy consultation, full installation and equipment supply across on-grid, off-grid and hybrid systems. Our SEDA-accredited engineers manage every stage in-house — site assessment, system design, TNB Net Energy Metering approvals, installation, commissioning and ongoing monitoring — so you deal with one accountable team and a system engineered for maximum yield and return on investment. Rooted in the East Coast and trusted across the region, we treat every installation as a 25-year relationship, not a one-off sale.

Our Mission

To make reliable, cost-effective solar energy accessible to every Malaysian home and business — cutting energy bills and carbon emissions alike.

Our Vision

To be Malaysia's most trusted solar partner, accelerating the nation's shift toward clean, self-generated power.

Our Services

Residential Solar Installation

Custom rooftop systems for homes and condominiums. We handle the full TNB NEM application, installation and commissioning — with flexible monthly payment plans so savings can start from day one.

Commercial Solar Solutions

Scalable solar PV for SMEs, offices, retail and factories. Lower your operating costs, lock in predictable energy pricing, and support green-building certification.

Industrial & Utility-Scale Solar

Large ground-mount and rooftop systems for industrial parks, warehouses and government facilities — delivered as full EPC contracts with performance guarantees.

Battery Energy Storage (BESS)

Pair solar with smart storage to maximise self-consumption, keep critical loads running through outages, and move toward true energy independence.

RE Consultation & Site Assessment

Free renewable-energy consultation, site survey, shadow analysis and ROI modelling — so you get the right on-grid, off-grid or hybrid setup before committing to anything.

O&M & Monitoring Services

24/7 remote monitoring, preventive maintenance, panel cleaning and inverter servicing keep your system performing at its peak, year after year.
